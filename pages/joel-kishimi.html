My DLES 2022 Experience At The Federal Housing Group Location
Article by Joel Kishimi (Programme Principal)
My name is Kishimi Joel AKA De-New-Kish-Joe. I am an undergraduate and a student of Federal Polytechnic Kaura Namoda, the Department of Food Technology I was privileged for the Deeper Life Entrepreneurship School [DLES 2022]. It was indeed an awesome experience, seeing myself being used by my maker to serve the purposes of the kingdom and to depopulate the kingdom of darkness and populate the kingdom of God by pulling the youth from the hand of the devil, breaking the yoke of addictions and helping them find purpose in life and destiny. Though, it was a huge sacrifice for me to shut down other activities for the past three weeks to always avail myself daily in the programme to teach the student but I count it as a great privilege to serve God in this capacity because it gives me great joy to see young boys and girls serving the purposes of the kingdom and walking in the fullness of their eternal ordination. I really appreciate the name of the Lord for the success of the DLES 2022 to see Jesus revealed in the life of all the participants and to see Jesus glorified. I also want to thank God for the corporation of all the instruments God used in one way or the other to impact into the lives of the students [academics session, leadership session, youth series and creativity]. I want to use this medium to thank bro Andrew in particular for devoting his time and attention for more than a week coming to impact the life of the children to know the essence and importance of coding and digital marketing. May the Almighty God reward your labour of love. Finally, I will like to say thank you to all our group leaders and members from Federal Housing Group, Ungwan Tanko Group and Karatudu Hausa Group for their support and contribution in monetary terms for the success of the DLES 2022. May your labour of love never go unrewarded.
