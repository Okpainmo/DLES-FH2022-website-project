My DLES 2022 Experience At The Federal Housing Group Location
Article by Joshua Okpanachi
I am Joshua Okpanachi by name. I’m an undergraduate and a member of the Deeper Christian Life Ministry. I would like to talk about my experience at the ongoing DLES. DLES popularly known as Deeper Life Entrepreneurship School is a yearly programme organized by the Deeper Christian Life Ministry to empower youths; young adult and undergraduates to be independent, self-employed and successful entrepreneurs. I’ve heard several announcements from the church moderator and pastor concerning the programme and I was determined to attend. Boom! The programme started. I wasn’t present the first day. I was disappointed. It was time for Monday Bible study and members of the church were assembled at the church premises for the service. Later on. I met a brother who attended. The Day 1 of the DLES. He shared his view about the programme and he also informed me that a programmer came to church that day and did introduction on the basics of coding. I was propelled and motivated by his words and I told myself I won’t miss that of tomorrow. The next day came, I was present and we were taught stuffs ranging from education, counsel series, temperament and lot more. It was time for coding, I listened attentively as the programmer take us through an educative section of coding. We were taught the meaning, uses and methods of coding. More so, we were introduced into simple and common programming language like HTML, CSS and Java Script. The good part of it is that our coach [programmer] promised to take us through a free mentorship for coding if only we are interested. This year’s package of DLES was educative, inspiring, informative and power-packed as our spiritual lives were awakened. Indeed, I got value!
