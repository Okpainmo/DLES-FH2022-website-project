My DLES 2022 Experience At The Federal Housing Group Location
Article by Davida Adeshina
Hi, my name is Davida Adeshina, telling you about my DLES 2022 experience. The first time I came for the programme, I saw it wasn’t as lively as success camp so I thought “it’s not going to be interesting at all”. They taught English, Maths. We had leadership counsel sessions and coding class. It was actually interesting and I actually liked it, well, except for the part that we had to write tests. The youth counsel series help make a teachable moment for me but it was the leadership counsel sessions that taught me a very valuable lesson, for example, when we were talking about public speaking it helped me for the fact that I would be speaking as the new assistant head girl. It was an eye-opener for me and I hope it will be an eye-opener for others in Jesus name. P.s the coding was the most magnificent part of this programme which was very helpful
