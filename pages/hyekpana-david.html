My DLES 2022 Experience At The Federal Housing Group Location
Article by Hyekpana David
My name is Hyekpana David from Borno State. Some time ago, someone told my mum about this programme held by Deeper Life Bible Church. I was very angry when my mum told me to go because I couldn’t imagine myself going for a programme every day, but she was my mum and I had to obey her, not knowing I was going into the experience of a lifetime. When I went, we were taught various kinds of things like teaching us on our academics, bible lesson, crafts and so on. There were much fun and I enjoyed it, and found my purpose to serve God more. I want to thank Deeper Life Bible Church for this wonderful programme they held for us ad for devoting their time to teach us more of God’s word, and also teaching us more on our academic. Thank you!
