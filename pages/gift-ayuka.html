My DLES 2022 Experience At The Federal Housing Group Location
Article by Gift Ndaks
My name is Gift Ayuka. I am here to share some experience that I learnt in this very impact. First, I have learnt on things youth are supposed to do and things not to do. Our youth coordinators told us that no matter many obstacles on the way we should not give up. So many youths are living a sinful live and committing all manners of sin today. They taught us to obey elders and have respect for them. They have impacted so many things upon us. We should not be lazy we should be hard working many things they told us. And another thing is that we need to be baptized by the Holy Ghost. Sincerely, sinful life is not the best life to live. People think that sin is the best place to live but no! if you have Jesus you have everything. People think if you follow Jesus is very hard, no. Our leaders taught us Maths, English and some other important subject we need to know. Thanks for reading and thanks to our leaders who created time and taught us. May God help them in Jesus name, Amen.
